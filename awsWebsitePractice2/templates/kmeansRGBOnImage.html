Article on image compression

How to use program:
1. Upload an image you want to do kmeans RGB clustering on. It has to be a jpg or png.
2. Enter the number of clusters/colors you want to represent the image. If you want to try a bunch of different clusters all at once, enter 0 for the number of clusters.
3. Press the submit button.
